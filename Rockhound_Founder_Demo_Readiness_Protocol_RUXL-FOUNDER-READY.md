## Human-Readable: Founder Demo Readiness Protocol (Ultra-Dense)

### Mindset Calibration

- Visualize success, embrace calm focus, accept minor glitches

### Pacing

- Steady, unhurried, pause at transitions, allow audience reactions

### Voice Rhythm

- Clear, moderate pace, intentional pauses, varied tone

### Device Handling

- Practice handoffs, keep devices close, minimize fumbling

### Slide Transitions

- Cue with narration, glance ahead, avoid abruptness

### Fallback Communication

- Acknowledge issues calmly, explain fallback, resume flow without apology

### Q&A Handling

- Listen fully, repeat/paraphrase, respond concisely, defer if needed

### Confidence Reinforcement

- Recall past wins, affirm Rockhound's value, trust preparation

### Pre-Demo Psychological Warm-Up

- 3 deep breaths, smile/stand tall, mentally rehearse opening, visualize positive response

### Acceptance Criteria

- Calm, focused, confident founder; steady, clear, engaging delivery; smooth transitions; graceful fallbacks; poised Q&A; positive, energized start
