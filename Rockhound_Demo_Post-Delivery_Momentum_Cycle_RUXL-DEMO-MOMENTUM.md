## Human-Readable: Demo Post-Delivery Momentum Cycle (Ultra-Dense)

### Follow-Up Communication

- Thank-you emails to attendees (24h)
- Personalized follow-ups for key leads/investors/partners
- Share highlights and next steps

### Lead Capture

- Lead form on landing page
- Prompt sign-ups for updates/early access
- Tag/segment leads by interest

### Outreach Sequences

- Targeted investor recap/metrics
- User waitlist/beta invites
- Partner collaboration proposals

### Demo Asset Repackaging

- Edit/publish demo recording (full/highlights)
- Shareable screenshots/GIFs
- Public demo deck PDF

### Social Amplification

- Announce demo success on all channels
- Share assets (video, screenshots, deck)
- Encourage audience sharing/tagging

### Landing Page Updates

- Add demo highlights/testimonials
- Demo video/deck download links
- Refresh CTA and lead capture

### 7-Day Momentum Plan

- Day 1: Follow-up emails, update landing, publish video
- Day 2: Targeted outreach, share highlights
- Day 3: Engage leads, respond to feedback
- Day 4: Release deck PDF, post testimonials
- Day 5: Q&A/AMA, progress update
- Day 6: Nurture leads, schedule calls
- Day 7: Review metrics, plan next milestone

### Acceptance Criteria

- All comms sent on schedule, leads engaged, assets repackaged/distributed, social amplified, landing updated, 7-day plan executed without drift
