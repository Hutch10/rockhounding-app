## Human-Readable: Founder Demo Activation Cycle (Ultra-Dense)

### Cognitive Warm-Up

- Review script/flows, visualize success, affirm calm focus

### Voice & Pacing Warm-Up

- Practice opening lines, run transitions with pauses, vary tone/pace

### Device Handling Muscle Memory

- Physically rehearse handoffs/screens, practice device navigation, simulate quick recovery

### Confidence Anchoring

- Recall past wins, affirm Rockhound's value, trust preparation

### Fallback Mindset

- Accept minor issues, prepare to pivot, keep focus on narrative

### Narrative Control Stance

- Maintain flow control, guide back to key points, use transitions to reinforce story

### Q&A Stance

- Listen fully, respond concisely/honestly, defer complex answers

### Morning-Of Activation Routine

- 3 deep breaths, smile/stand tall, mentally rehearse opening, check devices/reset data/review script, visualize positive response

### Acceptance Criteria

- Founder is mentally/physically prepared, voice/pacing smooth, device handling fluid, confidence visible, fallback mindset ready, narrative/Q&A stance clear, morning routine complete
