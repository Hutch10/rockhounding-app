# Lapidary Founder Operating Cycle — Year 1 Activation Plan (L‑FOC‑Y1)

## Purpose

Deterministically activate the founder operating cycle for a full 12-month horizon, ensuring continuous governance, evolution, ecosystem, category-leadership, and founder presence postures, with explicit founder syntheses and cadences.

## Annual Structure

- **Annual Governance Posture:** Founder sets and publishes governance principles, escalation protocols, and integrity standards for the year.
- **Annual Evolution Posture:** Founder defines evolution priorities, blueprint update cadence, and self-improvement targets.
- **Annual Ecosystem Posture:** Founder establishes ecosystem engagement, partnership, and category expansion goals.
- **Annual Category-Leadership Posture:** Founder articulates category vision, language propagation, and stewardship commitments.
- **Annual Founder Presence Posture:** Founder commits to public narrative, presence rituals, and cadence templates for the year.
- **Annual Founder Synthesis:** Founder leads a comprehensive synthesis session, recalibrates the operating baseline, and sets strategic direction for Lapidary and Rockhound.

## Quarterly Cycle

- **Quarterly Founder Synthesis:** Founder reviews governance, evolution, ecosystem, and category-leadership postures; recalibrates targets and lock-in rules; publishes quarterly synthesis and direction.
- **Quarterly Governance/Evolution Review:** Audit and harden governance and evolution blueprints; resolve escalations; update canon index.
- **Quarterly Ecosystem/Category Review:** Evaluate ecosystem growth, category language propagation, and stewardship impact.
- **Quarterly Founder Presence Review:** Audit public narrative, cadence adherence, and presence rituals.

## Monthly Cadence

- **Monthly Founder Cadence:** Founder reviews and signs off on all governance, evolution, ecosystem, and category-leadership checkpoints.
- **Monthly Synthesis:** Founder synthesizes progress, resolves escalations, and updates public narrative.
- **Monthly Presence Ritual:** Founder executes presence ritual and cadence template; updates public channels.

## Weekly Leadership Rhythm

- **Weekly Leadership Review:** Founder reviews audit logs, drift/escalation events, and integrity signals.
- **Weekly Blueprint/Activation Sync:** Confirm all blueprints and activation plans remain aligned; escalate mismatches.
- **Weekly Ecosystem/Category Pulse:** Monitor ecosystem signals, category language, and stewardship actions.
- **Weekly Founder Presence:** Founder executes weekly presence routine and narrative update.

## Automation & Verification

- All posture, synthesis, and cadence events are logged and versioned.
- Escalations auto-route to founder dashboard for review and resolution.
- All public narrative and cadence updates are cross-linked to canonical sources.
- Founder signoff required for all quarterly, monthly, and weekly syntheses.

## Founder-Level Oversight Actions

- Annual, quarterly, monthly, and weekly review and signoff on all posture, synthesis, cadence, and escalation events.
- Annual founder synthesis sets strategic direction and recalibrates all operating baselines for Lapidary and Rockhound.

---

**This L‑FOC‑Y1 plan ensures deterministic, founder-driven activation and oversight of all operating cycles for Lapidary and Rockhound across the full first year.**
